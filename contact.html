What to expect
01

You send me a note.

A few sentences about your business, what you are trying to build, and what is getting in the way. No form to fill out. No wrong way to reach out.

02

We get on a call.

Usually 30 to 45 minutes. I ask questions. I listen more than I talk. We figure out whether there is a real problem here for me to help with.

03

I send you a proposal, or I don't.

If I am the right fit, you get a written proposal with clear scope and pricing. If I am not, I will tell you and point you toward someone who might be.

Good fits

I do my best work with people who care deeply about what they are building.

Likely a fit

  • Small business owners or coaches with real expertise who need a system behind it
  • Multi-site operators who need their work to hold across locations
  • Educators and trainers wanting to move from delivering sessions to building systems
  • Anyone who would rather build something that lasts than ship something fast

Probably not a fit

  • One-off requests for templates, worksheets, or content without strategy
  • Projects where the outcome is already fully defined and you just need hands
  • Work that requires compromising on learning design quality to move faster

If you are not sure whether your project is a fit, write me anyway. An honest no is still a useful conversation.

The Reframe That Changes Everything
One shift in how you see your expertise changes what gets built, what it's worth, and what people say about it.

Here is the thing most business owners get wrong. They treat their knowledge like something to be shared. Post it, email it, talk about it, give it away and hope people remember them.

And that works — to a point. But sharing is not the same as designing. The difference between those two things is the difference between content that gets consumed and an experience that actually changes someone.
